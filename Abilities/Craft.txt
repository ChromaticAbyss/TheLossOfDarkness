====== Craft ======
Created Mittwoch 26 August 2015

The character can craft items from scrap and other raw materials, repair equipment and prevent wear.

=== Attributes ===
**Dexterity**, Intelligence



=== Incompetent ===
-

=== Beginner ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 1, Intelligence 0
**Cost**: 5 PP


=== Intermediate ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 2, Intelligence 1
**Cost**: 10 PP


=== Experienced ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 3, Intelligence 2
**Cost**: 20 PP


=== Professional ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 4, Intelligence 2
**Cost**: 40 PP


=== Legend ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 4, Intelligence 3 (One of these 6)
**Cost**: 80 PP




=== Specialisations ===
**Eye for quality**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Awareness 3
**Cost**: 5 PP
Passive
The character can easily gauge any items craftsmanship. Gain a +2 modifier to related Awareness skill checks.

**Careful work**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Dexterity 3
**Cost**: 7 PP
Passive
On a failed craft reroll the check with a -2 modifier. On a success nothing negative happens except the loss of time.

**Cook**
**Requirements**: Beginner, Awareness & Intelligence 1
**Cost**: 2 PP
Passive
The character is a good cook and can create very tasty meals given proper ingredients.

**Metalworker**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Endurance 2
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
Gains a +1 Modifier when working with bulky metal, especially welding, construction and making melee weapons.

**Woodworker**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Endurance 1
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
Gains a +1 Modifier when working with wood, includes construction and weaponmaking.

**Textile/Leatherworker**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Dexterity 1
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
Gains a +1 Modifier when crafting clothing and armor as well as other items from textile or leather.

**Gunmaker**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Intelligence 2
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
Gains a +1 Modifier when working on guns.

**Electrician**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Intelligence 3
**Cost**:10 PP
Passive
Can understand and work on complicated electrical circuits.

**Bionics**
**Requirements**: Experience, Intelligence 4, Electrician, Medic Intermediate
**Cost**:10 PP
Passive
Can understand and work on bionic implants and limbs.


=== Character Generation Modifiers ===
**Starting Equipment**: 
Beginner: You start with 20 TU worth of common items you could have crafted yourself.
Intermediate: You start with 50 TU worth of common items you could have crafted yourself.
Experienced: You start with 100 TU worth of common items you could have crafted yourself or traded for.
Professional: You start with 200 TU worth of common items you could have crafted yourself or traded for.
