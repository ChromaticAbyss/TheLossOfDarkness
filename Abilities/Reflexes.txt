====== Reflexes ======
Created Donnerstag 17 September 2015

Reflexes is a defensive ability to counter melee attacks and thrown weapons. 
In case no one has made a plan using Tactics the highest Reflexes will determine which party starts.


=== Attributes ===
**Dexterity**, Burst


=== Incompetent ===
-

=== Beginner ===
**Requirements**: Burst 1, Dexterity 2
**Cost**: 5 PP


=== Intermediate ===
**Requirements**: Burst 2, Dexterity 3
**Cost**: 10 PP


=== Experienced ===
**Requirements**: Burst 3, Dexterity 4
**Cost**: 20 PP


=== Professional ===
**Requirements**: Burst 4, Dexterity 5
**Cost**: 40 PP


=== Legend ===
**Requirements**: Burst 5, Dexterity 6
**Cost**: 80 PP




=== Specialisations ===
**Chosen one:**
Costs: 7PP, Requires Skilllevel: Experienced
Passive
You do not get any penalty for using Reflexes to ward off melee attacks while surrounded by any amount of enemies.

**First Strike:**
Costs: 5PP, Requires Skillevel: Intermediate
Passive
You gain a +1 modifier when rolling Reflexes to determine initiative.


=== Character Generation Modifiers ===
**Starting Equipment**: 
Beginner: You start with a basic melee weapon.
Intermediate: You start with a basic melee weapon.
Experienced: You start with a melee weapon worth 60 TU.
Professional: You start with a melee weapon worth 100 TU or less, this weapon has high quality and deals +1 damage.
