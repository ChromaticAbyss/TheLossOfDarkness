====== Cast ======
Created Saturday 05 September 2015

Cast is a mental ability that allows safer casting under higher amounts of [[Glow]]. This is a must have ability for characters that seek to solve many problems by using their Bindings, especially if they have mayn [[BurnTable|burn table]] entries.

=== Attributes ===
**Intelligence**, Awareness



=== Incompetent ===
-

=== Beginner ===
**Requirements**: Intelligence 2, Awareness 2, At least one Binding
**Cost**: 5 PP


=== Intermediate ===
**Requirements**:  Intelligence 3, Awareness 3
**Cost**: 10 PP


=== Experienced ===
**Requirements**: Intelligence 4, Awareness 4
**Cost**: 20 PP


=== Professional ===
**Requirements**:  Intelligence 5, Awareness 5
**Cost**: 40 PP


=== Legend ===
**Requirements**:  Intelligence 6, Awareness 6
**Cost**: 80 PP




=== Specialisations ===
**Glow Threshold**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Willpower 3
**Cost**: 10 PP
Passive
Grants +1 maximum Glow Threshold, which allows more casting until checks are needed.

**Glow Threshold(2)**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Willpower 4, Glow Threshold
**Cost**: 10 PP
Passive
Grants another +1 maximum Glow Threshold, which allows more casting until checks are needed.

**Overdrive**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Willpower 3
**Cost**: 5 PP
Passive
You have +1 to all Binding Masteries while you are at 40 [[Glow]] or more. This increase applies to all calculations!


=== Character Generation Modifiers ===
**Starting Equipment**: Intermediate+: You may start with 1D6 doses of medium quality [[Items:Healing And Drugs:Thunder|Thunder]].
