====== Binding ======
Created Saturday 05 September 2015

The character is trained in the art of binding which allows him to more safely aquire bindings in a drawn out process that is part science and part ritual.

=== Attributes ===
**Intelligence, Willpower,** Awareness


=== Incompetent ===
-
An incompetent person cannot use this ability except on character generation where a +0 is used.

=== Beginner ===
**Requirements**: Willpower 2, Intelligence 2, Awareness 1
**Cost**: 5 PP


=== Intermediate ===
**Requirements**: Willpower 3, Intelligence 3, Awareness 1, At least one Binding.
**Cost**: 10 PP


=== Experienced ===
**Requirements**: Willpower 4, Intelligence 4, Awareness 2, At least two Bindings.
**Cost**: 20 PP


=== Professional ===
**Requirements**: Willpower 5, Intelligence 5, Awareness 3, At least three Bindings.
**Cost**: 40 PP


=== Legend ===
**Requirements**: Willpower 6, Intelligence 6, Awareness 4, At least four Bindings.
**Cost**: 80 PP




=== Specialisations ===
**Symbiosis**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Awareness 5
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
When obtaining new Bindings you gain +2 to the Mastery level.


**Assistance**
**Requirements**: Experienced, Awareness 3
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
You can provide the Binding ability for another person by assisting them. This grants allows them to use the following points instead of their own:
Experienced: 2
Professional: 4
Legend: 6


**Exorcist**
**Requirements**: Experienced, Willpower 5
**Cost**:10 PP
Passive
When binding an unbound to yourself against its will the difficulty is no longer increased by one category.


=== Character Generation Modifiers ===
**Background**: A character needs to have some background explaination as to why he possesses this rare ability which is commonly illegal to practice.
