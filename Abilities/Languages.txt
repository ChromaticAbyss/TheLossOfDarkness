====== Language ======
Created Freitag 18 September 2015

Languages have two tiers:
Basic and Fluid





=== Attributes ===
**Intelligence**



=== Incompetent ===
Character knows the language of the area he grew up in 

=== Beginner ===
**Requirements**: Intelligence 2
**Cost**: 5 PP


=== Intermediate ===
**Requirements**: Intelligence 3, Dexterity 3, one of them 4
**Cost**: 10 PP


=== Experienced ===
**Requirements**: Intelligence 4, Dexterity 4, one of them 5
**Cost**: 20 PP


=== Professional ===
**Requirements**: Intelligence 5, Dexterity 5
**Cost**: 40 PP


=== Legend ===
**Requirements**: Intelligence 6, Dexterity 6
**Cost**: 80 PP




=== Specialisations ===
**Patch them up**
**Requirements**: Intermediate
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
Stopping a bleeding only takes 3 actions. 


**Lifesaver**
**Requirements**: Experienced
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
When performing **Emergency** **Stabilization **the maximum reduction in duration you can achieve is now 8.  


**Vital Operation**
**Requirements**: Experienced
**Cost**:5 PP
Takes 1 hour
Requires: Somewhat sterile enviournment, Operation instruments, some form of anesthesia(**vey hard** skill check if a component is missing).
Attempt to bring a person that is **stable** but in negative vitality up to 0 health. This is a **hard** check, the amount of leftover points is added to the targets vitality(maximum to 0). 
On a failure the target loses the **stable** status and starts losing vitality again until they can be stabilized again.
Attribute used is the minimum of Dexterity and Intelligence.

**Get well soon**
**Requirements**: Intermediate
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
When you successfully care for a persons wound they can roll a check for healing it every 3 days instead of every 7.



**Apothecary**
**Requirements**: Intermediate
**Cost**:3 PP
Passive
Gain a +2 modifier for all medicine/drug related Medicine checks. 
You gain a +1 bonus when using Medicine to give a bonus to any Medic related skill check (e.g. healing powder).
