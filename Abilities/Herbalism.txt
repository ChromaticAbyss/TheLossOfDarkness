====== Herbalism ======
Created Saturday 05 September 2015

Knowledge of edible, medical and dangerous plants is very valuable out in the wilderness and can easily make the difference between life and death.
A skilled herbalist knows his current enviournment and what useful plants to look for in it and also has ways to categorize previously unknown plant life. 



=== Attributes ===
**Awareness**, **Intelligence**

=== Specialisations ===
**Gatherer**
**Requirements**: Beginner, Awareness 3
**Cost**: 5 PP
Passive
Gain +1 when actively searching for plants. 
+2 instead if just searching for edible plants.
