====== Drive ======
Created Montag 14 Dezember 2015

The ability to drive different kinds of vehicles.
This includes both land vehicles and boats.

=== Attributes ===
**Dexterity**, Awareness, Intelligence



=== Incompetent ===
-
Can pilot a rowing boat or bicycle at most.

=== Beginner ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 1, Awareness 0
**Cost**: 5 PP
Can drive basic vehicles at slow speeds without endangering themselves or others.

=== Intermediate ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 2, Awareness 1
**Cost**: 10 PP
Can drive slightly more complicated vehicles(manual transmission etc.) and faster.

=== Experienced ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 3, Awareness 1, Intelligence 2
**Cost**: 20 PP
Can perform difficult manouvers with different vehicles such as 180Â° fast turns, drifts and dodges.

=== Professional ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 3,Awareness 3, Intelligence 3
**Cost**: 40 PP
Can drive complicated vehicles such as war machines. Strong grasp at what most machines can do and can not do.

=== Legend ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 3,Awareness 3, Intelligence 3 (one of these 6)
**Cost**: 80 PP
Can drive basically anything and intuitively grasp the controls and capabilities of unknown vehicles. Can pull off nerve wrecking moves pretty reliably.

=== Character Generation Modifiers ===
**Starting Equipment**: 
Intermediate: You may start with a motorcycle or small car at half the usual cost.
Experienced:  You start with a motorcycle, motorboat or small car.
Professional: You start with a car.
