====== Scouting ======
Created Saturday 05 September 2015

This ability is used to actively search for dangers and estimate them before encountering them.
Its also used to look out for the best way and find interesting sites nearby.


=== Attributes ===
**Awareness, **Dexterity



=== Incompetent ===
-

=== Beginner ===
**Requirements**: Awareness 2
**Cost**: 5 PP


=== Intermediate ===
**Requirements**: Awareness 3
**Cost**: 10 PP


=== Experienced ===
**Requirements**: Awareness 4, Dexterity 2
**Cost**: 20 PP


=== Professional ===
**Requirements**: Awareness 5, Dexterity 3
**Cost**: 40 PP


=== Legend ===
**Requirements**: Awareness 6, Dexterity 3
**Cost**: 80 PP




=== Specialisations ===
**Unseen scouting**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Dexterity 3, Intermediate Stealth
**Cost**: 5 PP
Passive
While scouting you are by default moving stealthily.





=== Character Generation Modifiers ===
**Starting Equipment**: 
Intermediate: A low quality set of binoculars(+1 modifier, low durability, requires common mainainance)
Experienced: A medium quality set of binoculars(+1 modifier)
Professional: A high quality set of binoculars(+1 modifier, doesn't give off light reflexes that can betray your presence)
