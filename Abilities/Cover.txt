====== Cover ======
Created Saturday 05 September 2015

A characters ability to seek cover in a fight while in the best case still participating in it.

=== Attributes ===
**Dexterity**, Awareness



=== Incompetent ===
-

=== Beginner ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 1, Awareness 0 (one of them 2)
**Cost**: 5 PP


=== Intermediate ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 1, Awareness 1 (one of them 3)
**Cost**: 10 PP


=== Experienced ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 2, Awareness 2 (one of them 3)
**Cost**: 20 PP


=== Professional ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 3, Awareness 3 (one of them 4)
**Cost**: 40 PP


=== Legend ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 4, Awareness 4 (one of them 6)
**Cost**: 80 PP




=== Specialisations ===
**Survival Instinct**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Awareness 3
**Cost**: 5 PP
Passive
Increases the maximum cover effectiveness any object can give to you by one point.


**Covered Advance**
**Requirements**: Intermediate, Awareness and Dexterity 3
**Cost**: 5 PP
Passive
You may use a move action while covered if the terrain allows for it. This reduces your previous cover rating by 1 each time you do this.


**Evasive Movements**
**Requirements**: Experienced, Dexterity 4
**Cost**: 5 PP
Passive
Move in patterns that make you a bad target. You gain a Cover rating of 2 when using this as your Move Action(you can move up to half the regular distance doing this).


=== Character Generation Modifiers ===
**Starting Equipment**: 
Experienced+: You start with 1D3 medium quality [[Items:Weapons(Throwing):SmokeGrenade|Smoke Grenades]].
