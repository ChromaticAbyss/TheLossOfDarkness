====== Medic ======
Created Saturday 05 September 2015


**Stop bleeding**
5 Actions
Stopping a wound from bleeding requires little in terms of material(some fabric), actual pressure bandages are much better and grant a +3 modifier.
This checks difficulty varies on the location of the bleeding.
Leg, Arm: **Easy**
Torso: **Medium**
Head: **Hard**
Attribute used is Dexterity

**Woundcare**
5 minutes
Takes basic care of a wound to reduce the chances of infection and increase chances of fast healing.
Leg, Arm: **Medium**
Head, Torso: **Hard**
On a success the wound won't infect and the healing check gains a bonus equal to all leftover points over 3.
Attribute used is Intelligence


**Emergency** **Stabilization**
15 Actions
Stabilizing a **dieing** person ( <0 hp) to stop further hp loss is way more difficult then stopping a bleeding and requires a **hard** skill check.
Its usually best to take care of ongoing bleeding first in some way.
If you succeed with leftover points the duration drops by those points(maximum reduction is 5).
On a success the **dieing** process stops until further damage is recieved(bleeding does not count), a lot of care is needed when transporting a person in such state as even small mistakes can turn a person critical again.
Attribute used is the minimum of Dexterity and Intelligence.

**Patch them up**
**Requirements**: Intermediate
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
Stopping a bleeding only takes 3 actions. 


**Lifesaver**
**Requirements**: Experienced
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
When performing **Emergency** **Stabilization **the maximum reduction in duration you can achieve is now 8.  


**Vital Operation**
**Requirements**: Experienced
**Cost**:5 PP
Takes 1 hour
Requires: Somewhat sterile enviournment, Operation instruments, some form of anesthesia(**vey hard** skill check if a component is missing).
Attempt to bring a person that is **stable** but in negative vitality up to 0 health. This is a **hard** check, the amount of leftover points is added to the targets vitality(maximum to 0). 
On a failure the target loses the **stable** status and starts losing vitality again until they can be stabilized again.
Attribute used is the minimum of Dexterity and Intelligence.

**Get well soon**
**Requirements**: Intermediate
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
When you successfully care for a persons wound they can roll a check for healing it every 3 days instead of every 7.



**Apothecary**
**Requirements**: Intermediate
**Cost**:3 PP
Passive
Gain a +2 modifier for all medicine/drug related Medicine checks. 
You gain a +1 bonus when using Medicine to give a bonus to any Medic related skill check (e.g. healing powder).
