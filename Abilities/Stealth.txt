====== Stealth ======
Created Sonntag 06 September 2015

Summarises a characters ability to move around unnoticed, stay hidden and enter places he should not be in.

=== Attributes ===
**Dexterity**, Awareness



=== Incompetent ===
-

=== Beginner ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 2, Awareness 1
**Cost**: 5 PP


=== Intermediate ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 2, Awareness 2
**Cost**: 10 PP


=== Experienced ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 3, Awareness 2
**Cost**: 20 PP


=== Professional ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 4, Awareness 3
**Cost**: 40 PP


=== Legend ===
**Requirements**: Dexterity 6, Awareness 5
**Cost**: 80 PP


=== Specialisations ===
Infiltrator


Pickpocket


Blend in crowd



=== Character Generation Modifiers ===
**Starting Equipment**: 
Beginner: -
Intermediate: You start with a stolen item worth up to 70 TU.
Experienced: You start with a stolen item worth up to 130 TU and other traded items or currency worth up to 20 TU.
Professional: You start with a stolen item worth up to 150 TU and other traded items or currency worth up to 40 TU.
