====== Scavenging ======
Created Sonntag 06 September 2015

The character knows valuable remains from junk, knows which places might hold what you seek for and which places are most likely plundered already.

=== Attributes ===
**Awareness,** Intelligence



=== Incompetent ===
-

=== Beginner ===
**Requirements**: Awareness 1, Intelligence 0
**Cost**: 5 PP


=== Intermediate ===
**Requirements**: Awareness 2, Intelligence 2
**Cost**: 10 PP


=== Experienced ===
**Requirements**: Awareness 2, Intelligence 3
**Cost**: 20 PP


=== Professional ===
**Requirements**: Awareness 3, Intelligence 4
**Cost**: 40 PP


=== Legend ===
**Requirements**: Awareness 6, Intelligence 4 OR Awareness 5, Intelligence 5
**Cost**: 80 PP
