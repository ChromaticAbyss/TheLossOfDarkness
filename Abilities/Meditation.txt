====== Meditation ======
Created Donnerstag 17 September 2015

Similar to the Medic skill Meditation is used to restore lost Stability. The main differences to that skill is that its less common and it can only treat the person itself by default.

**Meditation Session**
2 hours absolutely undisturbed
Only once a day. Roll a a meditation check that is **normal** on more than 5 [[Attributes:Stability|Stability]] and **hard** when at 5 or below.
On a success regain one additional point of stability.





=== Attributes ===
**Willpower**, Awareness, Intelligence

-

**Cost**: 80 PP




=== Specialisations ===
**Quick Trance**
**Requirements**: Intermediate
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
Meditation only takes 1 hour.


**Serenity**
**Requirements**: Experienced
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
Can meditate up to twice a day


**Deep Trance**
**Requirements**: Experienced
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
Regenerate +1 Stability on successfull meditation.


**Psychodoc**
**Requirements**: Experienced
**Cost**:10 PP
Passive
Can treat other people, roll that check on Awareness and make it a category harder.
This doesn't benefit the character itself and does **not** benefit from **Quick Trance, Serenity **or **Deep Trance.**
The per day limit is per patient not per doc.

**Professional** **Psychodoc**
**Requirements**: Professional,**Psychodoc**
**Cost**:5 PP
Passive
Treating others is no longer a category harder. 





=== Character Generation Modifiers ===
