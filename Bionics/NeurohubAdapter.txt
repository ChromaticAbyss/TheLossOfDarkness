====== NeurohubAdapter ======
Created Sunday 28 June 2015

This is an adapter located in one brain hemisphere that allows connecting two brain based Bionics.

Slots: Brain(1)
Plasticity cost: 2
Grants two additional slots for Brain Bionics. The functionality of these rests completely on this implant doing its job properly.
