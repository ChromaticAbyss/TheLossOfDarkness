====== Receptacle ======
Created Tuesday 30 June 2015

Slots: Chest
Plasticity cost: 1

A Bionic upgrade that can hold a standart Binding cube. While you have one slotted you gain +2 to the respective [[Binding:BindingMastery|Mastery]] and unlock the baseline cast if you don't have it already. The contained binding cubes can be exchanged which takes 1 hour.
