====== ReflexBooster ======
Created Sunday 28 June 2015

Slots: Eye(1),Spine,Brain(1)
Plasticity cost: 2

Allows dueling with against shooting attacks using Reflexes , this is a **hard** check. 
Grants a +2 modifier to the Reflexes skill.
