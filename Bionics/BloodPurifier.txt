====== BloodPurifier ======
Created Sunday 28 June 2015

Slots: Chest
Plasticity cost: 1

Grants a +2 modifier to the Resistance skill when dealing with poison. There are variants that do and some that don't make the character immune to alcohol and lesser drugs.
This implant can replace a kidney too.
