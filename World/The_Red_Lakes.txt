====== The Red Lakes ======
Created Sunday 12 July 2015

The red lakes are a series of artificial lakes around which there is fertile soil. Soil and lakes provide sustainance for multiple major cities that together form the **Red Lake Autonomy.** It is ruled from the council of the 44 in [[World:The Red Lakes:Hydrogen City|Hydrogen City]].
