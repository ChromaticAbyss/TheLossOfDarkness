====== Family Reffa ======
Created Freitag 18 September 2015



The Reffa family owns the fish processing plant and trawlers on the red lakes. In addition to controling this major source of food they are tied to pretty much every inn and brothel in Carbo city. 


=== Values of the organisation ===
**Quality of Life**: Even in those dire times life must be worth living for everyone!


=== Property of the organisation ===
Fish Processing Plant
Fishing trawlers
6 Inns/motels
2 Brothels
