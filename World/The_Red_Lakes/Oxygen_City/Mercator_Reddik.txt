====== Mercator Reddik ======
Created Mittwoch 07 Oktober 2015

Mercator Reddik is the [[World:Religions:Purity|Impensus Munditus]] of the Red Bay Area, residing in **Oxygen City**. He gives flaming sermons twice a week here in the **Great Cenotaph** in the city centre. Once a week he travels to **Litihum City ** to preach to the soldiers there.
