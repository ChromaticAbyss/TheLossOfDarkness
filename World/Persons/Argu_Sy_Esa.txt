====== Argu Sy Esa ======
Created Donnerstag 27 August 2015

Another person that isn't seen on the streets much, Argu is rumored to be a master of intrigue and the art of binding.
Tough he shows no signs of mutation he seems teeming with power. Eyewittnesses confirm him possessing a vast arsenal
of binding derived powers.
