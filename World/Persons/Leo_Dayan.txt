====== Leo Dayan ======
Created Saturday 19 December 2015

Leo Dayan was a [[World:Religions:Purity|Munditus]] in [[World:The Red Lakes:Oxygen City|The Fortress(Oxygen]]City), a few months after the events around the **Godless Three** he went to [[World:Persons:Giles Sa Vera|Giles Sa Vera]] for a lengthy discussion and shortly after abandoned his faith and was last seen leaving towards the [LNK]Pyroclasm.
He left a few notes that caused him to be branded a heretic, not that it mattered because he never reappeared.
