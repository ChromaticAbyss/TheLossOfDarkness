====== Kyrne Esta Estras ======
Created Donnerstag 27 August 2015

The Grandmaster of the Forge Kyrne is never seen anywhere without at least a bulletproof vest and an armored car full of reinforcements nearby.
The paranoid woman recently overtook a significant portion of the production facilities in Silicium city(Link).
Kyrne is not known for many words and instead lets actions speak. 
It is also rumored that she has a heartrate sensor linked to the detonators of dozens of bombs hidden in her property.
