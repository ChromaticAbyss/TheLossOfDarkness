====== Melis Estra Haba ======
Created Saturday 05 September 2015

Melis is the woman in charge of controling all kinds of ware movements in the docks. People that have experienced her sister Theresia will thankfully note that she is much calmer than Theresia. She is also much less intelligent and mostly hands down any work to the people benath her.
