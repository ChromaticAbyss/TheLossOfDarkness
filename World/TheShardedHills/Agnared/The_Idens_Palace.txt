====== The Idens Palace ======
Created Saturday 26 December 2015

An old suite that seems to belong to a conjurer. Even the dimly lit parts seem to hurt in the eyes. 
Awareness checks with Spectrum: Reveals infinitely small lines of extreme brightness that form runes and circles on the walls.
