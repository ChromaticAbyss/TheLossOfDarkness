====== Resin ======
Created Dienstag 15 September 2015

A lot of trees(especially pines) produce a resin to close their wounds wich also makes a great glue when mixed with charcoal.

=== Biome ===
Wherever Trees grow

=== Collecting ===
**Check**: Herbalism + Awareness (easy)
**Duration**: 2 hours
**Time of Year**: Not Winter
**[Failure] **
Nothing
**[Success]**
1D3 Trees with lots of Resin
**[Success 5+]**
1D6 Trees with lots of Resin



=== Loot per plant ===
1 Resin/Glue (Making the glue requires a fire)
