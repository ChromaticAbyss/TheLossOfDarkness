====== AlageenaFlower ======
Created Saturday 05 September 2015

Alageena is a plant that grows in swampy areas and sprouts green/yellowish flowers in spring. It grows to about 1.4 metres tall and is pretty easy to find once in bloom.

=== Biome ===
Swamp


=== Collecting ===
**Check**: Herbalism + Awareness (easy)
**Duration**: 2 hours
**Time of Year**: Spring
**[Failure] **
Nothing
**[Success]**
4D6 Alageena flower leaves
**[Success 5+]**
4D20 Alageena flower leaves
