====== SpiritShrub ======
Created Donnerstag 01 Oktober 2015

Has a stench to it that can mask a persons natural smell pretty well. Since its not rare in the wilds this is a good protection against wild animals. It won't fool a guard dog in a city one bit tough.



=== Biome ===
Forest
Tundra
Mountains


=== Collecting ===
**Check**: Herbalism + Awareness (normal)
**Duration**: 2 hours
**Time of Year**: All
**[Failure] **
Nothing
**[Success]**
4D6 uses
**[Success 5+]**
4D20 uses
