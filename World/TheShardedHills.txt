====== TheShardedHills ======
Created Mittwoch 16 Dezember 2015

An extended treeless hill landscape that is overgrown with shrubs and cultivated to a large degree. Between the hill one of the most dominant kinds of landmark here is old ruins that rise from the enviournment.
Digging anywhere for more than a few spades will yield rocky rubble instead of nourishing soil.
