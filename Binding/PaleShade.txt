====== PaleShade ======
Created Thursday 25 June 2015


A pale entity that stalks the shadows and blends in crowds.


[[ProgressPoints|PP]]-cost on character generation: 20


**Binding Process:** 
[[Binding|Stability]]: Normal difficulty (-0)
[[Binding|Mutation:]] Normal difficulty (+0)  
		**Pale Skin**: Very pale almost white skin, does not take to direct sunlight very kindly(-1 modifier to everything while out in bright sun without cover).

[[Binding|Mutation:]] Normal difficulty (+0)  
	**          Mask for a Face**: This mutation consists of a botox like frozen face that looks very young but can not convey emotion very well. Included is a reduction of the ability to read others emotion as well, -2 to all checks in social situations.

[[Binding|Mutation:]] Normal difficulty (+5)  
	**          Whisper**: The characters voice is only a whisper. They can't talk loudly for long and can not scream at all.

[[Binding|Burn Table:]] Normal difficulty (+0)
		**Plans within plans:** Until your glow reaches 0 you cannot regain Stability.

[[Binding|Burn Table:]] Normal difficulty (+4)
		**Fade to oblivion:** See the effect of **Fade from memory** for the duration of [[Glow]] minutes. The difference is that nobody recognizes the character even if they know them for ages.



[[Binding|Amalgamation]]: Normal difficulty



**Specialisations/Casts:** 

**Fade from memory**
[Starting Cast]
Glow: 6 , 1 Action
Character becomes completely unmemorable even for persons that observed them up to [[BindingMastery:|BindingMastery]] minutes before.
		
**Camoflage**
Cost: 5 PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 5
Glow: 5, 3 Actions
Character starts blending into the current enviournment. This camoflage affects all senses, to detect the character the character a **hard** check on [[Attributes|Awareness]] is needed, this may be modified in addition by the remaining points of a [[Abilities:Stealth|stealth]] roll.
Lasts up to [[BindingMastery]] * 2 turns of combat but is broken by fast movement.
This ability does not have any effect on animals, machines, phantoms and other lesser intellects.
