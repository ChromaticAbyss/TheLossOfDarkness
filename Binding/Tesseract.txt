=== Specialisations/Casts ===
**Polydimensional Perception**
[Starting Cast]
Glow: 5, 1 Actions
The character can perceive into multiple dimensions with a range dependant on the characters [[BindingMastery]] and also percieve distortions in spacetime. This can be activated manually or when prompted.
**Passive: **The character can visualize higher dimensions very easily and intuitively work with them.



**Dimensional Shearing**
Cost: 10 PP, Requires BindingMastery: 5
Glow: 20, 1 Hour
The character marks a circular area up to the radius of [[BindingMastery]] metres with intricate runes. After this the marked area and everything in it can be shifted along any of the countless dimensions for a short distance. 
Usually the target areas bear some similarity to the actual world, kind of shadow worlds like **the Fade**.
