====== Drifter ======
Created Thursday 25 June 2015

												   **Drifter Binding**


An aimlessly drifting entity that sometimes binds to travellers of the wastes.


[[ProgressPoints|PP]]-cost on character generation: 20


**Binding Process:** 
[[Binding|Stability]]: Normal difficulty (-0)
[[Binding|Mutation:]] Normal difficulty (-7)  
		**Drifters Drive**: -1 modifier to all skills when staying longer than a week in one location.

[[Binding|Burn Table:]] Normal difficulty (+0)
		**Drifters Trance:** If this happens during action it equals a 1 turn stun. If it happens in a calm the driver just silently walks away for [[Glow]] minutes to gaze at the sky. If they are stopped from leaving they suffer from **Drifters Drive** for the duration.

[[Binding|Amalgamation]]: Normal difficulty



=== Specialisations/Casts ===
**We go where we go:** [Starting Cast]
			   Glow: 3 , 0 Actions(when promted)
			   Using this Cast you can add your [[BindingMastery|Mastery]] to a single Pathfinding or Tracking check.

**Walk it off**: Cost: 5 PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 5
			   Cast: Glow 7, can be cast on allies(costs 12 Glow if you do)
		 Any ailment you have does not reduce your travel speed for the next [[BindingMastery]] hours.
		 The target may also use their Meditation ability during this time.


			
**Fluency**: Cost: 3 PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 3
		 Cast: Glow: 5, 1 Actions
		 Cast this to gain fluency in a foreign language for as many minutes as you have [[Binding:BindingMastery|BindingMastery]].
			

**Lingering Fluency**: Cost: 5 PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 6
			   Passive
			   After casting **Fluency** you have a window of [[Binding:BindingMastery|BindingMastery/2]] hours where you understand the language of your choice but can't speak it. If you want to reactivate **Fluency** during this time it only costs 3 Glow.

**Unbroken:** Cost: 4 PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 8
		Cast: Glow 10, cannot be cast while near allies or above 5 health/stability
		This can only be cast when the drifter is all on his own and in a dire situation.
		When casting this a goal must be set that the drifter wants to accomplish(catch up to the group, revenge etc.), then this Specialisation is removed from the character.
		The character is instantly restored to 1 Vitality and Stability if below, can not regenerate health and stability over 5 while this runs.
		Every check related to staying alive and fulfilling the goal is one category **easier**.
		This condition ends on (re)joing a group, fulfilling the goal or death.


=== Low Stability ===

**Spirit of Wind**: The character seeks out new places immediately and will not stay at any one place for more than 5 minutes at a time. If the character is forced to stay at a location he loses 1 Stability per hour.
