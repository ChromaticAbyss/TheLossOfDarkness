====== BindingMastery ======
Created Thursday 25 June 2015

A binding mastery is like a normal Attribute in terms of rolling checks.
Usually a mastery improves the effects of all Casts for a Binding. It also unlocks further specializations into the Binding and new Casts.

Points of Binding Mastery can be bought later in the game for 15 PP
