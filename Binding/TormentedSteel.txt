====== TormentedSteel ======
Created Sunday 12 July 2015


**Binding Process:** 
[[Binding|Stability]]: Hard difficulty (-0) [only Medium if you have 3 or more Bionics]

[[Binding|Mutation:]] Hard difficulty (-5)  
**Machine like**: All movements become more machine like, less fluid.
-2 to [[Attributes|Dexterity]]
The following abilities gain a bonus of 1: Hand to Hand, Bionics, Craft(Metal,Engines)
		
[[Binding|Mutation:]] Hard difficulty (+0)  
**Steel Skin**: The characters skin produces steel armor plates. The thus hardened skin grants the character +1 [[CombatRules:Armor|Armor]]. However this skin isn't very flexible(-1 modifier to [[Attributes|Dexterity]]).
		
[[Binding|Mutation:]] Hard difficulty (+0)  
**Well oiled machine**: The character now has to consume oil regularly(1 ltr / week) or suffer a -1 modifer to all physical attirbutes. Also his sweat is replaced with oil. 
		


[[Binding|Amalgamation]]: Hard difficulty [only Medium if you have 3 or more Bionics]



=== Specialisations/Casts ===
**Beyond Flesh**
[Starting Ability]
 Passive
 You gain your [[Binding:BindingMastery|Mastery]] as [[Attributes:Plasticity|Plasticity]].

**Rust** 
Cost: 5 PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 3
Cast: Glow: 7, 1 Actions
A metallic object nearby starts rusting fast. This deals [[BindingMastery:|BindingMastery]] structural damage to it each turn of combat.
Certain surfaces and alloys may be resistant to rust and take only half or no damage at all.
Duration: [[BindingMastery:|BindingMastery]] turns of combat.

**Bionic Regeneration**
Costs:5PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 7
Passive
Your bionics and implants regenerate durability.
This happens at a rate of 1 point per sleep cycle, the points go to the lowest durability element first.		

**Cross Metal**
Costs:8PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 8
Glow: 8, 1 Actions
You can pass through metal for [[BindingMastery]] turns. If you end off inside metal with any body part this ability forcibly reactivates.
Weapons made of metal still have their normal effect on you.

**Lockdown**
Costs:8PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 4
Glow: target mass in kg * 2 , 2 Actions
Disbles a weapon or other technolgoical object with a mass of  [[BindingMastery:|BindingMastery]] ^2  kilograms or less for 10 turns of combat.
