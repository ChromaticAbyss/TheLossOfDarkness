====== TormentedSteel ======
Created Sunday 12 July 2015


**Binding Process:** 
[[Binding|Stability]]: Hard difficulty (-0) [only Medium if you have 3 or more Bionics]

[[Binding|Mutation:]] Hard difficulty (-5)  
		**Machine like**: All movements become more machine like, less fluid.
		-2 to Dexterity
		The following abilities gain a bonus of 1: Hand to Hand, Bionics, Craft(Metal,Engines)
		
[[Binding|Mutation:]] Hard difficulty (+0)  
		**Steel Skin**: The characters skin produces steel armor plates. The thus hardened skin grants the character +1 [[CombatRules:Armor|Armor]]. However this skin isn't very flexible(-1 modifier to Reflexes, Sprint and Obstacle).
		
[[Binding|Mutation:]] Hard difficulty (+0)  
		**Well oiled machine**: The character now has to consume oil regularly(1 ltr / week) or suffer a -1 modifer to all physical attirbutes. Also his sweat is replaced with oil. 
		


[[Binding|Amalgamation]]: Hard difficulty [only Medium if you have 3 or more Bionics]



=== Specialisations/Casts ===
**Beyond Flesh**
[Starting Ability]
 Passive
 You gain your [[Binding:BindingMastery|Mastery]] as Plasticity.
	


**Bionic Regeneration**
Costs:5PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 7
Passive
Your bionics and implants regenerate durability.
This happens at a rate of 1 point per sleep cycle, the points go to the lowest durability element first.
