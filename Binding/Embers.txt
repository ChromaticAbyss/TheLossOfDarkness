====== Embers ======
Created Wednesday 22 July 2015




[[ProgressPoints|PP]]-cost on character generation: 20


**Binding Process:** 
[[Binding|Stability]]: Normal difficulty (+0)
[[Binding|Mutation:]] Normal difficulty (+0)  
		**Inner Flame**: The character has a massively increased body temparature. This temperature is needed to sustain him however. The character takes double the effects from cold and half the effects of heat.

[[Binding|Mutation:]] Normal difficulty (+0)  
		**Pyromania**: The character has a desire to see things burn, this increases the lower his Stability goes. You can only use the Meditation ability while near a sufficiently large or interesting fire. 


[[Binding|Mutation:]] Normal difficulty (+4)  
		**Mouth of the Abyss**: The mouth and/or eyes of the character are constantly illuminated as if fire would burn within.

[[Binding|Mutation:]] Normal difficulty (+3)  
		**Emotional Addiction**: The character has very strong emotions like Rage,Fear etc.


[[Binding|Burn Table:]] Normal difficulty (+0)
		**Firestarter:** For [[Glow]] minutes small fires spontaneusly spawn around the character.

[[Binding|Burn Table:]] Normal difficulty (+0)

=== Specialisations/Casts ===
**Conjure Flame**
[Starting Cast]
Glow: 6 , 1 Action
Conjures a small flame up to 1 metre away. This flame can be used to ignite a campfire or to deal [[BindingMastery:|BindingMastery]] / 3 damage.


**Pyrokinesis**
Cost: 5 PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 6
Cast: Glow: 12, 2 Actions
Takes an existing fire and moves it around. This can be used for example to save a burning building or to deal damage to an enemy.
You can attack enemies with the fire during the duration which deals [[Binding:BindingMastery|BindingMastery]] / 2 damage each time.
The maximum range you can manipulate flames in is 10 metres.
Duration: [[BindingMastery:|BindingMastery]] / 2 turns of combat.

**Rust** 
Cost: 5 PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 3
Cast: Glow: 7, 1 Actions
A metallic object nearby starts rusting fast. This deals [[BindingMastery:|BindingMastery]] structural damage to it each turn of combat.
Certain surfaces and alloys may be resistant to rust and take only half or no damage at all.
Duration: [[BindingMastery:|BindingMastery]] turns of combat.


**Fireproof**
Cost: 5 PP, Requires [[BindingMastery:]] 5
Cast: Glow: 5, 1 Actions
You are immune to damage dealt by heat,fire,smoke and suffocation due to fire consuming your oxygen while in it.
Duration: [[BindingMastery:|BindingMastery]] minutes.


=== Low Stability ===
**Pyromania**: The character wants to set fire to as much stuff as possible and then watch the flames.
