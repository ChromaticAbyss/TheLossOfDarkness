====== Rust Storm ======
Created Sonntag 25 Oktober 2015

These are the devastated remains of Tormented Speel infused tribal warmachines.
They are rolling clouds or spinning tornados filled with dust and metal shards weighting up to 3 kilograms.


**Stats&Abilities:** 
Vitality/Stability:  20
Cloud Radius: 4 metres



=== Attack ===
Attacks by moving ontop of things and eroding them.

=== Special Abilities ===
**Tornado of the gritting waste**
Per turn of combat everyone within the cloud must roll a Hard Reflexes+Dexterity roll to avoid getting hit with scrap for 1D6 blunt damage.
Even if a character manages to not get hit their equipment takes 1 Structural Damage and they cannot breathe while in the cloud.
