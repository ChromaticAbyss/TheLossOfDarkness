====== Stats ======
Created Wednesday 24 June 2015

Stats to keep track of:
Vitality(Physical) 
	Default Maximum is 15
Stability(Mental) 
	Default Maximum is 15
[[Glow]](Goes up as you use your Binding abilities, cools down naturally) 
	Default trigger Threshold is 5
	Goes down 1 per hour if no Casts are used, not outside at night(Aura).
Plasticity(The characters brains ability to map to bionics)
	Default Maximum is 3, which means 3 simple implants can be handled
