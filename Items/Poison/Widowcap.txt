====== Widowcap ======
Created Donnerstag 17 September 2015

A bitter tasting mushroom that is pretty poisonous when eaten. Its a little difficult to hide the taste but if it succeeds it can prove extraordinarily deadly. 




=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: neglectible
**Durability**: -
**Est Value**: 2 TU

=== Crafting ===
By collecting them in nature and cooking 

=== Lesser Quality ===
**-**


=== Medium Quality ===
**Effect**: After an hour the victim takes 2 damage per hour until the end of the effect. Within the first 2 hours its possible to stop the effect by inducing vomiting.
**Duration**: 12 hours.
**Application**: as food poison


=== High Quality ===
**-**
