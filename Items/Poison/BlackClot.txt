====== BlackClot ======
Created Donnerstag 17 September 2015

Another blood poison that induces clotting and with that massive organ damage.



=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: neglectible
**Durability**: -
**Est Value**: 20 TU

=== Crafting ===
Fro minerals that can be prospected in the mountains
Crafting+Intelligence medium


=== Lesser Quality ===
**Effect**: After 1D6 turns of combat the target takes 1 damage per turn.
**Duration**: 1 Minute(10 turns of combat)
**Application**: weapon poison

=== Medium Quality ===
**Effect**: After 2 turns of combat the target takes 1 damage per turn.
**Duration**: 2 Minutes(20 turns of combat)
**Application**: weapon poison

=== High Quality ===
**Effect**: After 1 turns of combat the target takes 2 damage per turn.
**Duration**: 2 Minutes(20 turns of combat)
**Application**: weapon poison
