====== Curl ======
Created Donnerstag 17 September 2015

A blood poison that induces convulsion after a short time, effectively rendering the target incapacitated for the duration.


=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: neglectible
**Durability**: -
**Est Value**: 10 TU

=== Crafting ===
From snake or stingmouse venom, then some basic cooking and desitallation
Crafting+Intelligence medium


=== Lesser Quality ===
**Effect**: After 1D6 turns of combat the target suffers from pain(-1 all) and inability to control its muscles(additional -2 all physical attributes)
**Duration**: 2 minutes
**Application**: weapon poison

=== Medium Quality ===
**Effect**: After 1 turns of combat the target suffers from pain(-1 all) and inability to control its muscles(additional -2 all physical attributes)
**Duration**: 2 minutes
**Application**: weapon poison

=== High Quality ===
**Effect**: Instantly the target suffers from pain(-1 all) and inability to control its muscles(additional -2 all physical attributes)
**Duration**: 2 minutes
**Application**: weapon poison
