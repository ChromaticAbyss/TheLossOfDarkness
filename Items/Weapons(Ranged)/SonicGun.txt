====== SonicGun ======
Created Tuesday 07 July 2015

Screamer Sonic gun, equipped with a powerful compressor and culminator this gun can fire a powerful burst of sound. This can disorient and stun targets at mid range and deal serious damage on close range.

=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: 1.0kg
**Durability**: ?
**Est Value(Wilderness)**: 80 Army Rations (100 for the variant that takes charges)
**Est Value(Cities)**:  50 TU (70 for the Variant)

=== Combat Stats: ===
**Range**: 20 metres
**Damage**: 1D6+3-1 per metre distance to the target, damage from this weapon is not mitigated by armor.
		Target rolls a Reflexes(normal) check or is stunned for 1 turn(2 turns on targets closer than 10 metres).
**Reload**:
   **every X uses**: 5
**   takes:** 1 Action(check at start), weapon charges automatically for 1 min before its usable again.
**Recoil**: 2
**Size**: Small, Two Hands 

=== Ammunition: ===
Certain models can hold gas charges

**50ml tear gas charge**
	**Est Value(Cities)**: 5 TU
	Targets closer than 15 metres must also roll Resistance(normal) or take a -2 modifier to all rolls for 5 turns. This applies to everyone within 3 metres of the impact zone.

**50ml neurotoxin charge**
	**Rare**
	**Est Value(Cities)**: 30TU
	Target must roll a Resistance(hard) check or they gain the following condition for 1 hour(or until a fitting antidote is applied):
		**Paralyzed:** You lose the ability to move or breathe instantly. You take 1 damage per turn starting from 1 min in unless you get assisted breathing.
