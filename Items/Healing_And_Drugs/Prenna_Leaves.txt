====== Prenna Leaves ======
Created Saturday 05 September 2015

The leaves of this very plain looking shadow loving plant can be chewed to combat sleepyness.

=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: neglectible
**Durability**: -
**Est Value**: 2 TU

=== Crafting ===
By herbalism: 2 hours, Herbalism+Awareness (normal) Biome: Forest, Ruins, Mountains. Doses found: 1D6


=== Lesser Quality ===
**-**

=== Medium Quality ===
**Effect**: Supresses the effects of tiredness, these come back in full after the effect has ended.
**Side Effect**: -
**Duration**: 1 hour
**Addiction**: +2
**Applictation**: 5 Minutes

=== High Quality ===
**-**


=== Addiction ===
**Fall off**: 2/day
**Resistance Threshold**: 8
**Addiction Threshold**: 30
**Withdrawl**: An annoying tingling feeling and numb fingertips (-1 modifier to dexterity skill checks that have to do with the hands)
