====== WoundPowder ======
Created Sunday 09 August 2015

A pretty common powder or paste made from herbs to help the healing of wounds.


=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: neglectible
**Durability**: ?
**Est Value**: 5 Army Rations

=== Crafting Stats ===
**-**


=== Lesser Quality ===
**Effect**: While using the Woundcare ability you can apply this powder to get a +1 modifier.


=== Medium Quality ===
**Effect**: While using the Woundcare ability you can apply this powder to get a +2 modifier.

=== High Quality ===
**Effect**: While using the Woundcare ability you can apply this powder to get a +3 modifier. Also stops bleeding on a wound instantly.
