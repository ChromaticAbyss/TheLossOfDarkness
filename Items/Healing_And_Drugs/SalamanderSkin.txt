====== SalamanderSkin ======
Created Saturday 05 September 2015

The skin of certain salamanders can be fried with fat. The fat then can be consumed to obtain a calming effect and curb infections.
Lesser quality refers to a less potent and slightly more poisonous variant of the salamander which is easy to confuse.
This is an emergency medicine and poor peoples drug, the salamanders for which are usually caught by children in swamps.

=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: neglectible
**Durability**: -
**Est Value**: 4 TU

=== Crafting ===
[[World:Animals:YellowSalamander|By hunting]]


=== Lesser Quality ===
**Effect**: Grants +1 to all Endurance checks to fight infection. +1 to Meditate checks during the duration.
**Side Effect**: -1 Willpower
**Duration**: 6 hours
**Addiction**: +1
**Applictation**: 5 Minutes

=== Medium Quality ===
**Effect**: Grants +2 to all Endurance checks to fight infection, regenerates 1 stability per two hours.
**Side Effect**: roll a d6, on a 1,2,3: -1 Willpower 
**Duration**:12 hours
**Addiction**: +1
**Applictation**: 5 Minutes

=== High Quality ===
**-**


=== Addiction ===
**Fall off**: 1/day
**Resistance Threshold**: 2
**Addiction Threshold**: 10
**Withdrawl**: -1 Willpower
