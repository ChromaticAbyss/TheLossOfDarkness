====== Isotonic Solution ======
Created Tuesday 07 July 2015

A medic can use this solution to balance out strong bleeding and prevent an instant collapse due to pressure loss.

=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: 1kg
**Durability**: ?
**Est Value**: 1 Army Rations

=== Crafting Stats ===



=== Lesser Quality ===
**Effect**: Grants the target a puffer of 5 points from which half of the bleeding damage is taken until its consumed.
**Side Effect**: Deals one point of Vitality damage.
**Duration**: 1 hour
**Apllication Duration**: 2 Actions

=== Medium Quality ===
**Effect**: Grants the target a puffer of 10 points from which half of the bleeding damage is taken until its consumed.
**Side Effect**: -
**Duration**: 1 hour
**Apllication Duration**: 2 Actions
