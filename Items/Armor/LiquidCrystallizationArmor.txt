====== LiquidCrystallizationArmor ======
Created Saturday 08 August 2015

This refined product of the [[Binding:Crystalline|Crystalline Binding]] has the marvellous ability of being completely flexible in liquid form and under sudden impacts of any kind will instantly crystallize and harden into a formidable defense.



=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: 7 kg
**Durability**: ?
**Est Value(Wilderness)**: -
**Est Value(Cities)**: 1000 TU


=== Armor Stats ===
**Zones**: All 
**Pierce Armor**: 5
**Blunt Armor**: 5
**Cold Protection**: 1
**Radiation Protection**: 0
**Electrical Protection**: 1
**Mali**: -1
Downside: After taking a hit that deals 5 or more damage the armor hardens and is less flexible for 5 turns of combat. This increases the armor malus by -1 for the time, the timer is reset on each such impact.



=== Variants and Modifications ===
**Chest only**: 500 TU cost, maximum Malus: 3.
