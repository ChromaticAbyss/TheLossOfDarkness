====== ElectroArmor ======
Created Saturday 08 August 2015

The main defensive mechanism of this armor is deterrence. It shocks anyone who pierces it in melee potentially stunning them.


=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: 4kg
**Durability**: ?
**Est Value(Wilderness)**: -
**Est Value(Cities)**: 50 TU


=== Armor Stats ===
**Zones**: Vary, usually full
**Pierce Armor**: 2
**Blunt Armor**: 2
**Cold Protection**: 2
**Radiation Protection**: 1
**Electrical Protection**: 5
**Mali**: -1

For the electrical discharge roll 1D6. Half of that is applied as damage(zone doesn't matter). 
If the dice roll is equal to or greater than the targets wound threshold the victim then has to pass a Willpower normal(-the dice roll) check or is stunned for 2 turns of combat.


=== Ammunition: ===
**Battery:**
	**Est Value(Wilderness)**: 10 Army Rations
	**Est Value(Cities)**:  10 TU
	Contains 20 charges, each electrical shock consumes one charge.


=== Variants and Modifications ===
**Invisible**: The armor doesn't look much different from commonly worn leather/thick clothing. Price +50 TU.
