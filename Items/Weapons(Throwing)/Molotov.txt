====== Molotov ======
Created Wednesday 29 July 2015

A very simple weapon to ingite structures, persons and areas.

=== General Item Stats ===
**Wgt**: 0.5 kg
**Durability**: ?
**Est Value(Wilderness)**: 2 Army Rations (A bottle of liquor and some fabric)
**Est Value(Cities)**: 1 Trade Units (The same but its cheaper to get gasoline)


=== Crafting Stats ===
...

=== Combat Stats: ===
**Range**: 15 metres
**Damage**: Sprays everything within 2 metres with burning liquid. The zone and persons in it burn for for 1D3 of damage each turn of combat(roll once at the start). 
By expending an Action to roll on (nunburning) ground a person reduces the burning value on it by 1, an ally may help extinguish the flames.
Persons in the zone take the respective damage each turn (in addition if they got hit as well).
**Reload**:	-
**Recoil**: Not applicable
**Size**: Small, One Hand
**Cover can be used**: At half its value applied to the duel below
**Duel vs Reflexes:** Always(Dashing to safety)



**Gasoline instead of Liquor**
+1 to the burn intensity, burning zone can not be extinguished with water


**Adhesive Igniting agent**
**Est Value(Wilderness)**: 10 Army Rations
**Est Value(Cities)**: 5 Trade Units (for some sticky and flammable substances to mix)
To remove one point from the burn 2 actions are needed.
