====== Unarmed ======
Created Wednesday 08 July 2015

Deals 1 damage on straight punches etc. with intent to hurt but not outright kill.

Deals 1D3+1 damage on more visceral unarmed attacks to attempt to kill. e.g. slamming against a wall with full force, kicks in the side on a target thats already down.
