====== WoundThreshold ======
Created Dienstag 15 September 2015

Derived Stat
Starts at 5. A character with [[Attributes|Burst]] or [[Attributes|Endurance]] 5 or more gains +1 WoundThreshold.
A Value that determines how much damage needs to be inflict in a single strike to deal a wound to this character. Wounds inflict negative modifiers to related skill checks aswell as causing bleed and having the risk of infectiton.

Healing of wounds: Every 7 days after obtaining a wound you can roll for wound healing in your regeneration phase. 
This is a **Normal** check on a value of 7-the amount of wounds you have total. If you succeed you heal one wound (oldest wounds first).
 If you fail and the wound is not clean it will become infected.
