====== StatusEffects ======
Created Donnerstag 01 Oktober 2015

Exhausted: -1 modifier to Endurance
Tired: -1 Awareness, Intelligence (1 day without sleep)
Sleep Deprived: -2 Awareness, Intelligence, -1 Endurance, Willpower  (3+ days without sleep)
