====== Abilities ======
Created Mittwoch 26 August 2015

Ability roll: Add the requested Attribute(if none is specified the one in **bold**) with the modifier from you competence level and other modifiers to the ability/attribute.

=== Incompetent ===
Modifier: -3
You start at that level. At worst you automatically fail any check related to this, at best you get a -3 modifier.

=== Beginner ===
Modifier: 0
Cost Default: 5

=== Intermediate ===
Modifier: 3
Cost Default: 10


=== Experienced ===
Modifier: 6
Cost Default: 20


=== Professional ===
Modifier: 9
Cost Default: 40


=== Legend ===
Modifier: 12
Cost Default: 80
